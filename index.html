About the Author

Munashe Soka

Munashe Soka's writing ministry has experienced rapid growth since the release of his debut book, "The Hidden Man Of The Heart," in 2016. His subsequent titles have been met with widespread enthusiasm, resonating with an ever-expanding audience.

As a dedicated teacher of God's Word, Munashe's ministry is rooted in Bible-based teachings, faith, love, and a passion for soul-winning. His heart's desire is to spread the Gospel to diverse ethnic groups, transforming countless lives in the process.

Through his carefully crafted works, Munashe addresses critical issues facing the modern church, from financial stewardship and biblical giving to spiritual authority and the transformative power of grace. Each book combines scriptural insights, personal anecdotes, and practical guidance to empower readers in their faith journey.
